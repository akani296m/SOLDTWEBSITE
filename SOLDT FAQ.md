# **SOLDT FAQ**

---

## **Pricing**

### **What pricing plans does SOLDT offer?**

SOLDT currently offers two plans designed for different stages of business growth.

**Growth – R330/month**  
 Built for new and growing sellers. This plan includes everything you need to launch and run a professional online store.

**Pro – R799/month**  
 Designed for established sellers who need more advanced capabilities and higher limits across the SOLDT Suite.

Both plans include:

• Unlimited products  
 • Unlimited orders  
 • Access to the SOLDT Suite of built-in apps

The **Pro plan** also includes:

• Lower transaction fees  
 • Higher usage limits across SOLDT apps

---

### **How much does SOLDT cost?**

You can start selling online with SOLDT from **R330 per month**.

SOLDT charges a small **0.6% transaction fee on successful orders**, making it one of the most affordable commerce platforms available for South African merchants.

---

### **Are there limits on how many products or orders I can have?**

No.

Both SOLDT plans allow **unlimited products and unlimited orders**, so you can grow your business without worrying about platform limits.

---

## **Getting Started**

### **How quickly can I launch a store on SOLDT?**

Most merchants can launch their store in **under an hour**.

SOLDT was designed for fast setup. After signing up, you can add products, connect payments, and publish your store without installing apps or configuring complex integrations.

---

### **Do I need to be a web designer or developer to use SOLDT?**

No.

SOLDT was built so anyone can launch an online store without technical skills.

Your storefront is designed to look professional from day one.

For merchants who want more control, SOLDT also includes a **simple code editor** that allows you to customize your store.

---

### **What do I need to start selling on SOLDT?**

To start selling online you only need:

• A SOLDT account  
 • A product to sell

You can begin by signing up for a **free trial**, adding your products, and publishing your store.

---

### **Can I connect my own domain to SOLDT?**

Yes.

When you create a store you automatically receive a free **.mysoldtstore.co.za** domain.

You can also:

• Connect a domain purchased from a third-party domain provider  
 • Purchase and manage a domain directly from your SOLDT dashboard

### **Can I customize my online store?**

Yes.

## **Payments**

### **Can I connect my own payment processor?**

Yes.

SOLDT supports a wide range of **South African payment gateways** with pre-built integrations.

Connecting a payment provider takes only **a few minutes** and does not require any coding.

---

### **What payment gateways does SOLDT support?**

SOLDT integrates with several popular South African payment providers.

You can connect supported payment gateways directly from your dashboard using a simple setup process.

---

### **What currencies does SOLDT support?**

By default all SOLDT stores operate in **South African Rand (ZAR)**.

However merchants can also configure their store to sell in other currencies such as:

• USD  
 • EUR  
 • AUD

---

## **Orders & Shipping**

### **What happens when I receive an order?**

When a customer places an order you will receive:

• An **email notification**  
 • A **new order alert inside your dashboard**

Your **Orders page** provides everything you need to manage the order, including customer details, order items, and fulfillment status.

---

### **How does shipping work on SOLDT?**

SOLDT Shipping allows merchants to:

• Calculate shipping rates from supported carriers  
 • Create shipping waybills directly from the dashboard  
 • Track and manage deliveries

You can also use **manual fulfillment** if you prefer.

Once an order is fulfilled, your customer automatically receives shipping updates.

## **Free Trial & Billing**

### **What happens after my free trial ends?**

After your free trial ends you can choose the plan that best fits your business.

Your store, products, and data remain saved, so you can continue running your business without interruption.

If you decide not to continue, your store simply becomes inactive.

---

### **Is SOLDT suitable for beginners?**

Yes.

SOLDT was built to make starting an online business simple.

You don’t need coding skills or prior ecommerce experience. The platform handles the infrastructure so you can focus on building your store and selling your products.

---

### **How is SOLDT different from Shopify?**

SOLDT was built specifically for South African businesses.

Unlike global platforms that rely heavily on third-party apps, SOLDT includes the essential tools needed to run a store in South Africa directly inside the platform.

This allows merchants to launch faster, spend less on apps, and manage their entire business from one platform.
