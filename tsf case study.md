\# The Challenge

The Skin Farmacy operates in one of the most competitive categories in ecommerce — skincare.

In this market, presentation matters just as much as the product itself. Larger skincare brands often dominate online because their websites clearly show product results and build strong trust through reviews and visual storytelling.

The Skin Farmacy’s previous storefront made it difficult to compete with brands that had:

\- Highly polished product pages    
\- Visual before-and-after transformations    
\- Structured customer reviews    
\- Clear social proof  

Achieving this level of presentation on most ecommerce platforms often requires installing several apps or custom coding advanced product page layouts.

For a growing brand, this creates unnecessary complexity and cost.

The Skin Farmacy needed a simpler way to create \*\*high-converting skincare product pages without relying on multiple third-party tools.\*\*

\---

\# Why The Skin Farmacy Moved to SOLDT

The Skin Farmacy chose SOLDT because it allowed them to rebuild their storefront using tools already built into the platform.

\*\*SOLDT is an ecommerce platform designed for South African businesses, with storefronts, payments, and shipping built into one system.\*\*

Instead of installing several apps to achieve the functionality they needed, The Skin Farmacy could configure their store directly using SOLDT’s built-in storefront sections and review system.

This allowed the brand to focus on what matters most in skincare ecommerce — \*\*showing real product results and building customer trust.\*\*

\---

\# Showing Real Skincare Results

One of the most important additions to The Skin Farmacy’s new storefront was the ability to showcase real product transformations.

Using SOLDT’s \*\*image slider sections\*\*, The Skin Farmacy added before-and-after skincare images directly to their product pages.

These visual comparisons allow shoppers to immediately see the impact of the products.

For skincare buyers, seeing real transformations plays a major role in purchasing decisions. Instead of simply describing the product benefits, The Skin Farmacy could now \*\*show the results visually\*\*, increasing confidence for first-time buyers.

\---

\# Building Trust with Structured Reviews

Customer reviews are one of the most important trust signals in skincare ecommerce.

Using SOLDT’s \*\*built-in review tools\*\*, The Skin Farmacy was able to collect and display customer feedback in a more useful format.

Instead of displaying reviews in a generic list, customers could filter reviews based on skin type.

Examples include:

\- Oily skin    
\- Sensitive skin    
\- Acne-prone skin  

This allows shoppers to quickly find reviews from people with similar skin concerns, helping them feel more confident about trying the product themselves.

\---

\# Simplifying the Technology Stack

On many ecommerce platforms, achieving the same functionality would typically require installing multiple apps.

Brands often rely on tools such as:

\- Review apps like Yotpo    
\- Section or page builder apps    
\- Custom code to create specialized product layouts  

Managing several apps adds monthly costs and increases complexity for growing brands.

With SOLDT, the features The Skin Farmacy needed were already built into the platform. This allowed the brand to create a sophisticated product page experience without managing additional tools.

\# The Results

After launching their new storefront experience on SOLDT, The Skin Farmacy noticed improvements in how customers interacted with their product pages.

The biggest improvements came from:

\- Stronger visual storytelling    
\- Clearer social proof through structured reviews    
\- Easier navigation of relevant customer feedback  

Together, these changes helped increase buyer confidence during the purchase process and contributed to an improved conversion rate for the store.
