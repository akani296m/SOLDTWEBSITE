## What is a Payment Gateway?

A **payment gateway** is the technology that processes payments between your customer and your bank account.

When someone buys a product from your online store, the gateway:

1. Securely collects the customer's payment details
2. Communicates with the bank
3. Confirms the payment
4. Sends the money to your merchant account

Without a payment gateway, your online store **cannot accept payments online**.

## Best Payment Gateways for South African Online Stores

### PayFast

PayFast is one of the most popular payment gateways for South African ecommerce businesses. It supports a wide variety of payment methods and integrates with many online store platforms.

**Key features:**
- Visa & Mastercard payments
- Instant EFT from major SA banks
- QR payments
- Recurring billing options

**Best for:** Small to medium online stores that want a **simple, reliable gateway**.

---

### Peach Payments

Peach Payments is widely used by **larger ecommerce stores and startups** that want advanced payment infrastructure. It focuses heavily on scalability and international payments.

**Key features:**
- Card payments
- Apple Pay / Google Pay support
- International payments
- Fraud protection tools

**Best for:** Growing brands and stores that expect **high transaction volume**.

---

### Ozow

Ozow specialises in **Instant EFT payments**, allowing customers to pay directly from their bank account without using a card. This payment method is popular with South African shoppers who prefer not to enter card details online.

**Key features:**
- Instant EFT payments
- Real-time payment confirmation
- Bank-level security

**Best for:** Stores targeting customers who prefer **bank transfers instead of card payments**.

---

### Yoco

Yoco started as a point-of-sale provider but has expanded into **online payments for ecommerce businesses**. It provides both in-person and online payment solutions.

**Key features:**
- Online card payments
- POS hardware integration
- Business analytics tools

**Best for:** Businesses selling **both online and in physical locations**.

---

### PayGate

PayGate is a long-established payment provider with strong banking relationships in South Africa. It supports a wide range of payment types and currencies.

**Key features:**
- Local and international cards
- Multi-currency support
- Enterprise payment tools

**Best for:** Established businesses with **larger transaction volumes**.

## Typical Payment Gateway Fees in South Africa

Most payment gateways charge a **transaction fee per payment**.

| Payment Type | Typical Fee |
|---|---|
| Card payments | 2.5% – 3.5% |
| Instant EFT | 1.5% – 2.5% |

Fees vary depending on your monthly sales volume, payment type, and merchant risk profile. Some providers may also charge **setup or monthly fees**.

## What to Look for When Choosing a Payment Gateway

When evaluating payment gateways for your online store, consider the following:

**Transaction fees** — Lower fees mean higher profit margins on each sale.

**Settlement speed** — Some gateways pay merchants **daily**, while others settle payments weekly.

**Platform compatibility** — Your gateway must integrate smoothly with your ecommerce platform.
