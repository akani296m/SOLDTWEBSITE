Running an online store in South Africa comes with unique challenges — and unique opportunities. Here are five proven strategies that our most successful SOLDT merchants use.

## 1. Offer Multiple Payment Options

South African shoppers have strong preferences about how they pay. Some love card payments, others prefer EFT or SnapScan. The more options you offer, the fewer abandoned carts you'll see.

**Pro tip:** Enable at least 3 payment methods. Our data shows stores with 3+ payment options have a **23% higher conversion rate**.

## 2. Get Your Delivery Right

Nothing kills repeat purchases faster than a bad delivery experience. Partner with reliable local couriers and always provide tracking information.

With SOLDT, you get one-click integration with:
- **The Courier Guy** — great for nationwide delivery
- **Pargo** — perfect for collect-point pickup
- **Bob Go** — for comparing courier rates

## 3. Use WhatsApp for Everything

Your customers are already on WhatsApp. Use it for:
- Order confirmations
- Shipping updates
- Abandoned cart recovery (this alone can recover 15-20% of lost sales)
- Quick customer support

## 4. Invest in Product Photography

You don't need a professional studio. A clean background, natural lighting, and a smartphone camera can produce stunning product shots. Show your products from multiple angles and in context.

## 5. Tell Your Story

South African consumers love supporting local brands with authentic stories. Share your journey, your process, your team. Connect with customers on a human level.
