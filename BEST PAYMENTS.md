# **Best Payment Gateways for South African Online Stores (2026 Guide)**

If you're launching an online store in South Africa, one of the first things you'll need is a **payment gateway**.

A payment gateway allows your store to **securely accept payments from customers online** — whether they’re paying with a debit card, credit card, Instant EFT, or QR payment apps.

South Africa has several excellent payment providers, but choosing the right one can make a major difference to:

* your **checkout conversion rate**

* your **transaction fees**

* how quickly you **receive your payouts**

In this guide we'll break down the **best payment gateways for South African ecommerce stores**, how they work, and which one might be right for your business.

---

# **What is a Payment Gateway?**

A **payment gateway** is the technology that processes payments between your customer and your bank account.

When someone buys a product from your online store, the gateway:

1. securely collects the customer’s payment details

2. communicates with the bank

3. confirms the payment

4. sends the money to your merchant account

Without a payment gateway, your online store **cannot accept payments online**.

---

# **Popular Online Payment Methods in South Africa**

South African shoppers use a mix of payment methods depending on convenience and trust.

The most common ones include:

* **Credit and Debit Cards** (Visa & Mastercard)

* **Instant EFT**

* **QR Payments**

* **Buy Now Pay Later**

* **Digital Wallets**

The best payment gateways support **multiple payment options** so customers can pay using whatever method they prefer.

## **PayFast**

PayFast is one of the most popular payment gateways for South African ecommerce businesses.

It supports a wide variety of payment methods and integrates with many online store platforms.

**Key features**

* Visa & Mastercard payments

* Instant EFT from major SA banks

* QR payments

* Recurring billing options

**Best for**

Small to medium online stores that want a **simple, reliable gateway**.

---

## **Peach Payments**

Peach Payments is widely used by **larger ecommerce stores and startups** that want advanced payment infrastructure.

It focuses heavily on **scalability and international payments**.

**Key features**

* Card payments

* Apple Pay / Google Pay support

* international payments

* fraud protection tools

**Best for**

Growing brands and stores that expect **high transaction volume**.

---

## **Ozow**

Ozow specializes in **Instant EFT payments**, allowing customers to pay directly from their bank account without using a card.

This payment method is popular with South African shoppers who prefer **not to enter card details online**.

**Key features**

* Instant EFT payments

* real-time payment confirmation

* bank-level security

**Best for**

Stores targeting customers who prefer **bank transfers instead of card payments**.

---

## **Yoco**

Yoco started as a point-of-sale provider but has expanded into **online payments for ecommerce businesses**.

It provides both **in-person and online payment solutions**.

**Key features**

* online card payments

* POS hardware integration

* business analytics tools

**Best for**

Businesses selling **both online and in physical locations**.

---

## **PayGate**

PayGate is a long-established payment provider with strong banking relationships in South Africa.

It supports a wide range of payment types and currencies.

**Key features**

* local and international cards

* multi-currency support

* enterprise payment tools

**Best for**

Established businesses with **larger transaction volumes**.

# **Typical Payment Gateway Fees in South Africa**

Most payment gateways charge a **transaction fee per payment**.

Typical ranges look like this:

| Payment Type | Typical Fee |
| ----- | ----- |
| Card payments | 2.5% – 3.5% |
| Instant EFT | 1.5% – 2.5% |
| QR payments | \~2% |

Fees vary depending on:

* monthly sales volume

* payment type

* merchant risk profile

Some providers may also charge **setup or monthly fees**.

Your gateway must integrate smoothly with your ecommerce platform.
